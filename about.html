Lost In Light

A platforming game where you use light to help you traverse the level and rid yourself of enemies.

MOVEMENTS:

You can walk and jump around to traverse the level. Use your mouse to control your flashlight.

ENEMIES:

Running into any of these enemies will cause you to lose a life.

Snakes wanders back and forth on platforms. Kill it by leading them into level hazards.

Zombies wander back and forth on platforms and are able to jump. Kill it by leading them into level hazards.

Spiders have the ability to climb walls and the ceilings rather than just platforms. Kill it by leading them into level hazards.

Bats, unlike the others, are not restricted to platforms and chase after the player and are able to fly through platforms.

The ghost is the final boss, kill it and you win the game. Unlike the other enemies, the ghost doesn't not chase after the light, instead, after gaining the final powerup, is chased away by light.

COLLECTABLES:

Picking this potion up will restore your hearts.

There's a total of three map pieces, one per room aside the last room. Picking up a map piece will unlock the next room.

There's a total of four gems, one per room. Picking up a gem will give you another power up.

Power Ups:

1. Double jump

2. Expand your vision

3. Invisibility on a countdown

4. Light chases away the ghost
