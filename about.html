Lost In Light

A platforming game where you use light to help you traverse the level and rid yourself of enemies.

Get The Game Here

MOVEMENTS:

You can walk and jump around to traverse the level. Use your mouse to control your flashlight.

First Flashlight
Final Flashlight

ENEMIES:

Running into any of these enemies will cause you to lose a life.

Snakes wanders back and forth on platforms. Kill it by leading them into level hazards.

Spiders have the ability to climb walls and the ceilings rather than just platforms. Kill it by leading them into level hazards.

Bats are unlike the others. They will chase after your flashlight and are able to fly through platforms. Be careful that they don't fly in your way!

The ghost is the final boss, kill it and you win the game. Unlike the other enemies, the ghost doesn't not chase after the light, instead, after gaining the final powerup, is chased away by light. Entering the boss level, you get your lives back and you need to use your powers or jump on its head to defeat it.

COLLECTABLES:

Picking this potion up will restore your hearts.

There's a total of two map pieces, one per room aside the last room. Picking up a map piece will unlock the next room.

There's a total of two gems, one per room. Picking up a gem will give you another power up.

Power Ups:

1. Double jump

2. Light flash stuns the ghost
